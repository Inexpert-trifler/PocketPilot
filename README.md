# 🌟 The Problem

Most students have absolutely no idea where their money goes.

Every month:

- Food orders 📦
- UPI payments 💸
- Shopping 🛒
- Subscriptions 🎵
- Travel 🚕
- Random impulse purchases 😭

Money disappears.

Bank statements exist.

But nobody actually understands them.

---

# 💡 Our Solution

PocketPilot transforms boring transaction history into actionable financial intelligence.

Simply upload:

✅ Bank Statements

✅ PDFs

✅ CSV Files

✅ Transaction Screenshots

✅ Receipts

And PocketPilot automatically:

- Categorizes expenses
- Detects subscriptions
- Tracks spending habits
- Generates financial insights
- Predicts future spending
- Helps achieve savings goals
